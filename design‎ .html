Design

My idea came from the second day of the start of the project. At first, Vincent and me thought that we could make a glowing bicycle with the neopixel but it was not wearable so we both went back home and thought about it. The second day I came up with a brilliant idea about that to make it a bicycle lamp, which solved the problem.

The things that are creative about it is that it will display different colors and brightness levels according to the ambient light.*
