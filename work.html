How it Works

First you power the Micro:bit on then, by default it is the color/colour sensing mode when it is bright it will turn off and when it is dark it will turn on.

If you press button b on the Micro:bit(the second one) it will change to rainbow mode, this mode will make the lamp change colour according to the angle of the Micro:bit.
